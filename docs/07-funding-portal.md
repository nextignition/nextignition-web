# Funding Portal Documentation

## Overview

The Funding Portal enables founders to submit pitch materials, track funding requests, and connect with interested investors.

## üéØ Features

### For Founders

#### Pitch Deck Upload
1. Navigate to Funding Portal > Pitch Upload
2. Click "Upload Pitch Deck"
3. Select PDF file (max 10MB)
4. Set visibility:
   - **Public**: Visible to all investors
   - **Private**: Only shared investors
5. Click "Upload"
6. Deck appears in portfolio

**Requirements:**
- PDF format only
- Maximum 10MB file size
- Recommended: 10-15 slides
- Clear, professional content

#### Pitch Video Upload
1. Navigate to Funding Portal > Pitch Video
2. Choose option:
   - **Record**: Record 2-minute video
   - **Upload**: Upload existing video
3. If recording:
   - Click "Start Recording"
   - Timer shows 2-minute limit
   - Click "Stop" when done
   - Preview before submitting
4. If uploading:
   - Select video file
   - Ensure under 2 minutes
5. Click "Submit"
6. Video appears in portfolio

**Requirements:**
- Maximum 2 minutes
- MP4 format recommended
- Clear audio quality
- Good lighting

#### Funding Status Tracker
1. Go to Funding Status (`/(tabs)/funding-status`)
2. View all funding requests:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting review
   - **Viewed**: Investors viewed
   - **Interested**: Interest expressed
   - **Meeting Scheduled**: Meetings set
   - **Funded**: Successfully funded

#### View Interested Investors
- List of investors who viewed pitch
- Investors who expressed interest
- Connection requests received
- Meeting requests

#### Investor Messages
- View messages from investors
- Reply to inquiries
- Manage conversation history
- Schedule follow-ups

#### Meeting Scheduler
- View scheduled meetings
- Accept/decline requests
- Reschedule if needed
- Add to calendar
- Join video calls

#### Funding Analytics
- **Views**: Total pitch views
- **Connections**: Investor connections
- **Meetings**: Scheduled meetings
- **Success Rate**: Funding success
- **Trends**: Engagement over time

### For Investors

#### Browse Pitch Decks
1. Go to Startup Discovery
2. View startup cards
3. Click "View Pitch Deck"
4. Embedded PDF viewer opens
5. Navigate through slides
6. Download (if permitted)

#### Watch Pitch Videos
1. Click "Watch Video" on startup card
2. Embedded player opens
3. Play/pause controls
4. Full-screen option
5. 2-minute pitch

#### Save/Bookmark Startups
- Click bookmark icon
- Save to "Saved" list
- Organize with tags
- Add notes

#### Pitch History
- View all startups reviewed
- Engagement stats:
  - View count
  - Time spent
  - Actions taken

## üìã Pitch Deck Best Practices

### Structure
1. **Problem** (1-2 slides)
   - Clear problem statement
   - Market pain points
   - Why it matters

2. **Solution** (2-3 slides)
   - Your solution
   - How it works
   - Unique value proposition

3. **Market** (1-2 slides)
   - Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM)
   - Target customers
   - Market trends

4. **Business Model** (1-2 slides)
   - Revenue streams
   - Pricing strategy
   - Unit economics

5. **Traction** (2-3 slides)
   - Key metrics
   - Growth charts
   - Customer testimonials
   - Partnerships

6. **Team** (1 slide)
   - Key team members
   - Relevant experience
   - Advisory board

7. **Financials** (1-2 slides)
   - Revenue projections
   - Key assumptions
   - Use of funds

8. **Ask** (1 slide)
   - Funding amount
   - Use of funds
   - Milestones

### Design Tips
- Keep it simple and clean
- Use consistent branding
- High-quality images
- Readable fonts
- Professional appearance

- Clear audio
- Professional appearance
- Eye contact

- Keep it under 2 minutes
- Be authentic
- Show personality
- End with clear ask
